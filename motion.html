Motion

The objective of this project was to create a structure that portrayed the motion of two everyday tools, a vegetable chopper and a gardening fork. It was created using the limitations of four board feet of popler to create the structure on a 11” x 15” x 2” base made with two ash peices joined with a ship lap. The project with precise joints enabling it to stay together without glue instead using a combination of dowels, splines and other joinery tecneaques.
